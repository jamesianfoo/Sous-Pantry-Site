FAQs

Questions, answered.

Is Sous Pantry free?

Yes — Sous Pantry is free to download and use, with an optional Pro plan for advanced features like AI weekly meal plans and Saved Recipes.

Does my pantry data stay private?

Yes. Your pantry and recipe data is yours alone — we never sell or share it with third parties.

How does the AI work?

Sous Pantry's AI looks at what you have in your pantry, your dietary preferences, and suggests personalised meals you can actually cook tonight.

How do I cancel Pro?

You can cancel anytime through your Apple App Store subscription settings. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.
